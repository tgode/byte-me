# Vacation Policy — Serbia

**Document:** Employee Vacation Policy  
**Country:** Serbia  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Version:** 2.0

---

## Annual Vacation Days

Every full-time employee in Serbia is entitled to a minimum of **20 working days** of paid annual vacation, as required by the Serbian Labour Law (Zakon o radu).

Additional days are granted based on seniority and years of service with the company:

| Years of Service | Additional Days | Total |
|---|---|---|
| Less than 5 years | 0 | 20 |
| 5–10 years | 3 | 23 |
| More than 10 years | 5 | 25 |

---

## Minimum Mandatory Usage

Employees must use **at least 12 working days** of vacation within the calendar year. The remaining days may be carried forward to the next year, but must be used by June 30 of the following year.

---

## Requesting Vacation

Vacation requests must be submitted to the direct manager:
- Up to 5 days: **5 working days** in advance
- More than 5 days: **15 working days** in advance

Requests are submitted through the HR portal or by email to hr@company.rs.

---

## Summer Vacation Coordination

Employees are encouraged to plan and communicate their summer vacation plans by March 31 each year to allow team scheduling. The company reserves the right to stagger vacation periods to maintain operational continuity.

---

## Public Holiday Overlap

If a public holiday falls during an employee's vacation period, the vacation day is not consumed; it is automatically returned to the employee's balance.

---

## Vacation Pay

Vacation pay is calculated based on the employee's average salary from the preceding 12 months, in accordance with Serbian Labour Law.

---

## Termination of Employment

Upon termination, unused vacation days are paid out at the employee's average daily wage.
