# Employee Benefits — Serbia

**Document:** Employee Benefits Guide  
**Country:** Serbia  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Health Insurance

The company provides supplementary private health insurance for all full-time employees in Serbia, in addition to the mandatory state health insurance (RFZO).

Private insurance covers:
- Specialist consultations without referral
- Diagnostic imaging (MRI, CT, ultrasound)
- Hospitalization in partner private hospitals
- Dental: 2 checkups per year; treatments covered up to 40,000 RSD per year
- Vision: annual exam; frames/lenses up to 15,000 RSD every two years

Dependents (spouse and children under 18) may be added to the private plan at a 30% co-payment of the additional premium.

---

## Meal Allowance (Topli Obrok)

Employees receive a daily meal allowance (topli obrok) of **400 RSD** per working day, paid monthly. This benefit is tax-exempt under Serbian law.

---

## Transportation Allowance

Public transportation expenses for commuting are reimbursed up to **4,000 RSD per month** upon submission of receipts or monthly transit pass costs.

---

## Mobile Phone Allowance

Employees in client-facing, management, or sales roles receive a mobile phone allowance of **2,000 RSD per month**.

---

## Fitness Benefit

The company reimburses gym, fitness, or sports club memberships up to **3,000 RSD per month** upon receipt submission.

---

## Professional Development

Each employee has an annual professional development budget of **60,000 RSD** for training, courses, and certifications. Manager approval is required for individual expenses exceeding 15,000 RSD.

---

## Pension (Dobrovoljno Penzijsko Osiguranje)

The company contributes 4% of the employee's gross salary to a voluntary pension fund (Stub III), which is also tax-advantaged for the employee.

---

## Parental Leave

- Maternity and parental leave: up to 365 days, funded by the state for the full duration in accordance with Serbian law
- Paternity leave: 5 working days paid at full salary by the employer
- Childcare leave: available for children under 3, in accordance with Serbian Labour Law

---

## Regres Za Godisnji Odmor (Vacation Bonus)

Employees receive a vacation bonus (regres) paid annually, typically in May/June, equal to **50% of the monthly minimum wage** as defined by Serbian law.
