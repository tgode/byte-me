# Sick Leave Policy — Albania

**Document:** Sick Leave Policy  
**Country:** Albania  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Sick Leave Entitlement

Employees in Albania are entitled to paid sick leave in accordance with the Albanian Labour Code.

- **Days 1–14:** Employer pays 75% of the employee's average daily wage
- **Days 15 and beyond:** The Social Insurance Institute (ISSH) covers 70% of the average wage

---

## Notification Procedure

Employees must notify their direct manager or the HR department **as soon as possible**, and no later than **2 hours after the scheduled start of the working day**.

Notification must include the expected duration of absence, if known.

---

## Medical Certificate

For absences up to 3 consecutive days, a self-declaration is sufficient.

For absences of 4 or more consecutive days, a medical certificate (raport mjekësor) issued by a licensed physician is mandatory.

The medical certificate must be submitted to HR within **3 working days** of returning to work.

---

## Extended Illness

In cases of prolonged illness exceeding 30 days, the employee should contact HR to discuss available options, including unpaid leave or long-term disability coverage.

---

## Sick Days During Vacation

If an employee falls ill during their annual vacation and provides a medical certificate, the sick days will not be counted against the vacation balance. The vacation days may be rescheduled.

---

## Return to Work

Upon returning after an illness of more than 5 days, the employee may be required to attend a brief return-to-work meeting with their manager and the occupational health advisor.
