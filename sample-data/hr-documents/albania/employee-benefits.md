# Employee Benefits — Albania

**Document:** Employee Benefits Guide  
**Country:** Albania  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Health Insurance

All full-time employees in Albania are enrolled in the company group health insurance plan starting from their first working day.

Coverage includes:
- Primary and specialist medical consultations
- Hospitalization and surgical procedures
- Prescription medications (50% reimbursement up to 5,000 ALL per month)
- Dental care: 2 routine checkups per year at no cost; treatments reimbursed up to 20,000 ALL per year
- Vision: one annual eye exam; eyeglasses or contact lenses reimbursed up to 10,000 ALL every two years

Family members (spouse and children up to age 18) may be added to the plan. The employee contributes 30% of the additional premium.

---

## Meal Allowance

Employees receive a daily meal allowance of **800 ALL** per working day, paid as a monthly supplement on the payroll.

---

## Transportation Allowance

Employees who commute more than 10 km one-way are entitled to a monthly transportation allowance of **3,000 ALL**.

---

## Gym and Wellness

The company subsidizes gym membership up to **2,500 ALL per month**. Employees must provide a receipt to the HR department for reimbursement.

---

## Mobile Phone Allowance

Employees in client-facing or senior roles receive a monthly mobile phone allowance of **1,500 ALL**.

---

## Professional Development

Each employee has an annual training budget of **50,000 ALL** for courses, certifications, and professional conferences. Approval from the direct manager is required for expenses above 10,000 ALL.

---

## Pension

The company contributes 3% of the employee's gross salary to a supplementary pension fund, in addition to the mandatory contributions required by Albanian law.

---

## Parental Leave

- Maternity leave: 365 days, of which the first 150 days are paid at 80% of salary
- Paternity leave: 10 working days paid at full salary
- Adoptive parent leave: same as maternity/paternity leave
