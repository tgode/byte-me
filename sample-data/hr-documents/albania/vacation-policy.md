# Vacation Policy — Albania

**Document:** Employee Vacation Policy  
**Country:** Albania  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Version:** 2.1

---

## Annual Vacation Days

Every full-time employee in Albania is entitled to **20 working days** of paid annual vacation per calendar year, as required by the Albanian Labour Code.

Employees who have completed more than 5 years of service receive **25 working days** per year.

Part-time employees receive vacation entitlement proportional to their working hours.

---

## Vacation Accrual

Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.67 days per month of employment.

New employees are eligible to use accrued vacation days after completing 6 months of continuous service.

Unused vacation days may be carried over to the following year, up to a maximum of **10 days**.

---

## Requesting Vacation

Employees must submit a vacation request to their direct manager **at least 5 working days** in advance for requests up to 5 days.

For vacation periods exceeding 5 consecutive days, requests must be submitted **at least 15 working days** in advance.

Requests are submitted via the HR portal or by email to the HR department.

---

## Summer and Winter Vacation

Employees are encouraged to take at least **10 consecutive working days** during the summer period (June–September).

The company closes for **5 working days** during the Christmas and New Year period (December 25 – January 1). These days are not deducted from the employee's vacation balance.

---

## Compensation for Unused Vacation

Unused vacation days above the 10-day carry-over limit are compensated financially at the end of the calendar year, calculated based on the employee's daily gross salary.

Upon termination of employment, all unused accrued vacation days are paid out in full.
