# Payroll and Salary Information

**Document:** Payroll Schedule and Salary Information  
**Applies To:** All employees  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Payroll Schedule

Salaries are paid on the **last working day of each calendar month**.

If the last day of the month falls on a weekend or public holiday, payment is made on the preceding working day.

---

## Payroll Components

Your monthly payslip includes:
- **Gross salary:** your agreed base salary
- **Allowances:** meal, transport, phone (where applicable)
- **Bonuses:** performance and project bonuses (when applicable)
- **Deductions:** mandatory social and health insurance contributions, personal income tax

---

## Tax and Social Contributions

### Albania
- Income tax is calculated on a progressive scale (0–23%)
- Social insurance contribution (employee): 9.5% of gross salary
- Health insurance contribution (employee): 1.7% of gross salary

### Serbia
- Income tax: 10% flat rate on net income above the non-taxable threshold
- Pension and disability insurance (employee): 14% of gross salary
- Health insurance (employee): 5.15% of gross salary
- Unemployment insurance (employee): 0.75% of gross salary

---

## Payslip Access

Digital payslips are available in the HR portal on the last working day of each month. Employees receive an email notification when payslips are ready.

---

## Salary Review

Annual salary reviews take place in **Q1 of each year** (January–March), with adjustments effective from **April 1**.

Performance-based increases are determined by the performance review completed in February/March.
