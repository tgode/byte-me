# Employee Onboarding Guide

**Document:** Onboarding Guide for New Employees  
**Applies To:** All new hires  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Welcome

Welcome to the company! This guide will help you navigate your first days and weeks with us.

---

## Before Your First Day

You will receive:
- A welcome email with your IT credentials
- Instructions for setting up your laptop (shipped or available at the office)
- Your manager's contact information
- An invitation to your onboarding schedule in Microsoft Teams

---

## First Day

- Your manager or a designated buddy will greet you
- You will receive your laptop, badge, and access cards
- HR will walk you through your employment contract, payroll setup, and benefits enrollment
- IT will configure your accounts: Microsoft 365, Teams, VPN, and internal tools

---

## First Week

| Day | Activity |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | HR orientation, IT setup, manager meeting |
| Day 2 | Company culture, values, and strategy presentation |
| Day 3 | Department introduction and team meetings |
| Day 4 | Systems and tools training |
| Day 5 | Review of role responsibilities and 30-60-90 day goals |

---

## Probationary Period

All new employees begin with a probationary period of **3 months** (extendable to 6 months by mutual agreement).

During this period:
- Monthly check-ins with your manager are required
- Performance feedback is provided at the end of months 1, 2, and 3

## Company Policies

All employees must read and acknowledge the following policies within the first 2 weeks:

1. Code of Conduct
2. IT Security Policy
3. Data Protection Policy (GDPR)
4. Remote Work Policy
5. Vacation and Leave Policy
6. Anti-Harassment Policy

Policies are available on the company intranet and SharePoint.

---

## Buddy Program

Every new employee is assigned a buddy — a colleague who can answer informal questions and help you settle in. Your buddy will reach out during your first week.
