# Equipment Request Policy

**Document:** IT Equipment and Office Equipment Request Policy  
**Applies To:** All employees  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Standard Equipment

All new employees receive the following standard IT equipment on their first day:
- Laptop (Windows or macOS, based on role requirements)
- Headset / headphones
- Mouse and keyboard
- Power adapter and necessary cables

---

## Equipment Request Process

To request additional or replacement equipment:

1. Submit a request via the IT Helpdesk portal or email it@company.com
2. Include the item description, business justification, and estimated cost
3. Requests under **EUR 200** are approved by the direct manager
4. Requests between **EUR 200–1,000** require department head approval
5. Requests above **EUR 1,000** require Finance and HR approval

---

## Standard Laptop Refresh

Laptops are refreshed on a **3-year cycle**. After 3 years of use, employees may request a new laptop through the standard equipment request process.

---

## Home Office Equipment

Employees approved for regular remote work may request home office equipment:
- External monitor: one monitor up to EUR 300 reimbursed
- Standing desk: reimbursement up to EUR 200 (one-time, after 6 months of employment)
- Ergonomic chair: reimbursement up to EUR 150 (one-time, after 6 months of employment)

Home office equipment reimbursements are submitted to HR with receipts.

---

## Mobile Phones

A company mobile phone is provided to employees in client-facing, sales, or management roles. All other employees receive a monthly mobile allowance (see Benefits Policy).

---

## Lost, Damaged, or Stolen Equipment

Employees must report lost, damaged, or stolen company equipment to IT and HR immediately.

- **Accidental damage:** company covers replacement cost
- **Negligence or misuse:** employee may be held liable for repair or replacement costs
- **Theft:** must be reported to the police; insurance covers replacement

---

## Equipment Return

All company equipment must be returned upon termination of employment. Failure to return equipment may result in deduction from the final paycheck.
