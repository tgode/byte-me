# Remote Work Policy

**Document:** Remote Work and Work-From-Home Policy  
**Applies To:** Albania, Serbia, and all global offices  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Overview

The company supports flexible working arrangements. Employees may work remotely for part of their working week, subject to manager approval and role suitability.

---

## Eligibility

Remote work is available to employees who:
- Have completed their probationary period (typically 3–6 months)
- Work in a role that does not require physical presence (e.g., manufacturing, on-site client services)
- Maintain satisfactory performance ratings

---

## Remote Work Days

Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week** without requiring formal approval beyond initial agreement with their manager.

Working remotely more than 2 days per week requires written approval from the department head and HR.

Fully remote work arrangements (5 days/week) are available for certain roles and must be approved by the Country HR Director.

---

## Core Hours

Regardless of location, all employees must be available and reachable during **core hours: 10:00–16:00 local time** on scheduled working days.

---

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop and necessary peripherals for remote work. Employees are responsible for maintaining a suitable and secure work environment at home.

Employees must connect via the company VPN when accessing internal systems remotely.

---

## Internet Allowance

Employees approved for regular remote work (at least 2 days/week) receive a monthly internet reimbursement of:
- Albania: 1,500 ALL per month
- Serbia: 1,500 RSD per month

---

## Health and Safety

Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and ergonomically appropriate home workspace. The company's health and safety guidelines apply to remote work locations.

---

## Requests and Changes

Remote work arrangements should be agreed with the direct manager and documented. Changes to the arrangement require 2 weeks' notice.

For questions, contact HR:

**Serbia:** hr@company.rs
