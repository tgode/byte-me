# Travel and Business Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Document:** Travel and Business Expense Reimbursement  
**Applies To:** All employees  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Overview

The company reimburses reasonable and necessary business travel expenses incurred in the course of performing your role.

---

## Pre-Approval

All business travel must be approved by your direct manager **before booking**.

Travel costing more than **EUR 500** also requires approval from the department head.

---

## Booking

Employees should book travel through the company's preferred travel provider where possible. Booking directly is permitted when the preferred provider is unavailable or more expensive.

---

## Reimbursable Expenses

| Category | Limit | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Economy flights (domestic) | Actual cost | Business class requires VP approval |
| Economy flights (international) | Actual cost | Business class for flights over 6 hours |
| Train | Actual cost (standard class) | First class if no standard available |
| Taxi / ride-sharing | Actual cost | For airport transfers and late-night travel |
| Hotel | EUR 150/night (EU), EUR 180/night (US/UK) | Single room, business-class hotel |
| Meals (full day) | EUR 50/day | With receipts |
| Meals (half day) | EUR 25/day | With receipts |
| Parking | Actual cost | Receipt required |
| Internet / roaming | Up to EUR 20/day | |

---

## Per Diem Allowance

When receipts are not available for meals, a flat per diem may be claimed:
- Albania: **2,500 ALL per day**
- Serbia: **2,000 RSD per day**
- EU countries: **EUR 40 per day**

---

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not reimbursed:
- Personal entertainment, minibar charges, room service
- Traffic fines and penalties
- Spouse/partner travel expenses
- Upgrades to business or first class (unless pre-approved)
- Lost or stolen personal items

---

## Expense Submission

Submit expense reports within **30 days** of travel completion.

Required documentation: original receipts, purpose of trip, attendees (for meals).

Submissions are made through the expense management system or emailed to finance@company.com.

---

## Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next monthly payroll cycle.
